Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Fishing Forecast app ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data We Collect

The Fishing Forecast app is designed to work offline-first. All data you enter is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

Data stored locally on your device includes:

  • Fishing trip log entries (location name, date, catch notes, photos)
  • Saved fishing spots (name, notes, GPS coordinates, photos)
  • Custom fish species you add
  • App preferences and settings

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account credentials
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Location data to our servers

3. Third-Party Services

The app connects to the following external services only when you are online:

  • Open-Meteo API (open-meteo.com) — weather forecast data. No personal data is sent; only your selected coordinates.
  • Wikipedia API (wikipedia.org) — fish species photos. No personal data is sent.
  • CartoDB/OpenStreetMap — map tiles for the fishing map. Standard tile requests only.
  • Radio Browser API (radio-browser.info) — online radio station directory. No personal data is sent.
  • Open-Meteo Geocoding API — location search. Only the search query is sent.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them if you have concerns.

4. Location / GPS

The app may request access to your device's GPS location only when you explicitly tap the GPS button to set a fishing spot or log entry location. This location data is stored only on your device and is never sent to us.

5. Photos

Photos you add to log entries or fishing spots are stored locally on your device as part of the app's data. They are never uploaded to any server.

6. Backup & Export

The app's backup feature exports your data as a JSON file saved on your device. You control where this file goes. We do not receive any backup data.

7. Notifications

If you enable fishing condition alerts, the app schedules local notifications on your device. No data is sent to external servers to generate these notifications.

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the Clear Data option in the app's Backup tab
  • Uninstalling the app from your device
  • Clearing app data from your device's Settings
